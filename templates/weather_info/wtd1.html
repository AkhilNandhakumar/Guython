When it's Hot:

What to do:

What NOT do to:

  • Wear clothing made of breathable materials such as linen
  • Stay hydrated- drink lots of water
  • Wear lots of sunscreen
  • Stay inside- there are lots fun activities that can be done inside, such as visiting an indoor skating rink, eating ice cream, going to the pool
  • Spend too much time outside- excess exposure to UV rays can be harmful to your body
  • Drink caffeinated beverages instead of water- it's very important to stay hydrated!
  • Leave plastic bottles in the car- BPA levels in plastic water bottles can increase when they have excess exposure from the sun
