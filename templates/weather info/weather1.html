The Sun

What are the different types of clouds and how are they classified?

Clouds are classified by the height at which they are in the Earth's atmosphere.






High clouds- 10,000-60,000 ft high

The sun keeps the Earth warm and lit up. Without the sun, the surface of the Earth would turn into an icy wasteland. As the Earth rotates around the Sun, the temperature on Earth changes in the form of changing seasons. During the summer, the sun is closer to Earth, where it gets warmer. On the other hand, during the winter, the sun is further away from the Earth, which causes temperatures to drop.
