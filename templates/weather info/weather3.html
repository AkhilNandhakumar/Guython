The Wind

Everyone has felt wind and seen its effects, but what exactly is wind and what does it do? Wind is the global movement of a collective of gases known as air. Wind is occurs when pressure differences form which results in air from high pressure to flow to low pressure.


Wind can be found almost everywhere around the world and is notoriously hard to predict. The craziest thing about wind is it actually occurs in space, too!
