Math

Math appears in our everyday lives, and is arguably the most important skill to learn in school.

Without math, our society couldn't function. How would farmers know how much wheat to plant? How would employers know how much to pay their workers? Though students learn simple math, some of the most complex and strange events in the world can be explained with math.
